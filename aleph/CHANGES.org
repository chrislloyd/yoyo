* Changelog
** 0.0.x
*** 0.0.2

Breaking changes:

- ~with-webserver~ renamed to ~with-server~
- ~:port~ and ~:aleph-opts~ now live under one ~:server-opts~ map

Migration:

0.0.1:

#+BEGIN_SRC clojure
  (with-webserver {:handler ...
                   :port 3000
                   :aleph-opts {:option-key "option-val"
                                ...}})
#+END_SRC

0.0.2:

#+BEGIN_SRC clojure
  (with-server {:handler ...
                :server-opts {:port 3000
                              :option-key "option-val"
                              ...}})
#+END_SRC
